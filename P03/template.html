Brief

As part of a course project, my team and I met with Makercube, a makerspace in Langley, BC to help determine design opportunities for improvement in either internal or client-facing operations.

After 2 months of research using methods including cultural probes and a co-design workshop, my team devised a digital solution that could help makercube in their pursuit of creating a community with greater knowledge sharing and training, as well as incentivizing members to gain skills at their own pace.
