Valence

Valence is an online educational resource with a target audience of DIY electronics enthusiasts. The company's business model relies on partnerships with manufacturing and retail organizations, as well as a paid membership that grants users access to exclusive content and heavier discounts on components.

Tasks

  1. DISCUSS: Community discussion forum
  2. BUY: Purchasing a membership
  3. LEARN: Tutorials and resources on specific topic areas
  4. BUILD: Simple projects to get beginners started
  5. Circuit Builder to calculate the resistance required in a given circuit

Branding
